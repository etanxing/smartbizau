# SmartBizAU - AI Tools for Australian Small Business

## Why This Project

This is an SEO content site targeting Australian sole traders and small business owners looking for AI tools. The goal is simple: publish useful articles, attract organic search traffic, and monetize through Google AdSense ads.

It's one track in a broader plan to build AI-autonomous income streams starting from $100 AUD. This site is the low-risk leg -- no capital at stake, just content and time. Once traffic builds up, affiliate links for recommended tools can add another revenue layer on top of ads.

## How It Works

- Each article targets a specific long-tail keyword Australian small business owners are searching for, like "AI bookkeeping apps that sync with Australian banks" or "free AI tools for sole traders"
- Articles are SEO-optimized with schema.org markup, canonical URLs, meta descriptions, and proper heading structure
- A scheduled task generates 10 new articles daily at 9am, so the site keeps growing without manual effort
- Deployed on Cloudflare Workers with GitHub auto-deploy -- push to repo and the site updates automatically
- Zero hosting cost
